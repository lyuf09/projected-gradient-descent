\title{First-Order Methods for Smooth Convex Optimization in Isabelle/HOL}
\author{Feier Lyu}
\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
This entry develops reusable Isabelle/HOL infrastructure for first-order
methods in smooth convex optimization. It formalizes gradient interfaces,
first-order convexity certificates, smooth quadratic upper bounds, descent
and telescoping arguments, projection inequalities, convergence guarantees
for gradient descent and projected gradient descent, projected-gradient
mapping optimality conditions, strong convexity, linear-rate estimates, and
basic quadratic examples.
\end{abstract}
